## 🧑‍💻 Who I Am

I'm a developer who lives at the intersection of **hardware and software**. My passion lies in building things that bridge the physical and digital world — from microcontroller-based projects to self-hosted infrastructure. I believe great engineering is about solving real-world problems with elegant, efficient solutions.

When I'm not flashing firmware or wiring up a breadboard, you'll probably find me playing sports or tinkering with my home lab.
